Descendent Selectors - parent descendent {}

Descendent selectors will match all descendents regardless of nesting within the parent.
Read as any parents (and all of their) descendents.
The following matches: div p {color: yellow}.

I am a paragraph inside div

Child Selectors - parent > child {}

Child selectors will only choose first child (nested within parent) of the parent.
Read as parent (who created) child.
The following matches: div > .child-pg { color: green}

I am inside div (child-pg)

Second paragraph inside the div (child-pg)

This paragraph is nested inside a section (grand child-pg)

I am outside the div (child-pg)

Adjacent Sibling Selectors - neighbor1 + neighbor2

Adjacent sibling selectors will only match the first adjacent neighbor (neighbor2) from neighbor1.
Read as neighbor1 (next to/and) neighbor2.
The following matches: parent + .sib-pg {color: purple}

1 I am a parag and parent#1 (parent)

2 I am a parag and p#1's sibling (sib-pg)

3 I am a parag and parent#2 (parent)

4 I am a parag and p#2's sibling (sib-pg)

5 I am a parag and p#2's sibling (sib-pg)

General Sibling Selectors - neighbor1 ~ neighbor2

General sibling selector will match all neighbors of type neighbor2 that are adjacent to neighbor1.
Read as all neighbor2s (next to) neighbor1.
The following matches parent1 ~ .sib-pg1 {color: darkgreen}

1 I am a parag and parent#1 (parent1)

2 I am a parag and p#1's sibling (sib-pg1)

3 I am a parag and parent#2 (parent1)

4 I am a parag and p#2's sibling (sib-pg1)

5 I am a parag and p#2's sibling (sib-pg1)
